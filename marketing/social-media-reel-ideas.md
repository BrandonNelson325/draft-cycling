# Social Media Reel Ideas

## "Day in the Life" Format
- Wake up → morning check-in notification → dashboard shows "rest day" → show yourself actually resting guilt-free because the AI said so. Cyclists love being told it's OK to rest.
- Wake up → dashboard shows today's workout → ride it → post-ride feedback → coach adjusts tomorrow. Show the full loop in 30 seconds.

## Problem/Solution Hooks
- "I used to pay $200/mo for a cycling coach..." then show the app doing the same thing — adapting plans, adjusting for fatigue, rescheduling around life
- "POV: you tell your AI coach you have a race Saturday" → show it rearranging your week automatically
- "You skipped a workout. A human coach judges you. An AI coach reschedules." → show the move workout flow

## Screen Recording Bangers
- Real-time chat with the AI coach asking it to build a training plan — fast-forward the conversation, show the calendar filling up. The "wow" moment is instant.
- "Watch me plan 8 weeks of training in 60 seconds" — screen record the plan builder flow
- Show asking the coach "I only have 45 minutes today" and it picking the perfect workout

## Relatable Cycling Content (with app tie-in)
- "Types of cyclists who need an AI coach" — the overtrained guy, the "I just ride with no plan" guy, the one who does sweet spot every day
- "What your TSB says about you" — use the freshness gauge as the visual
- "Rest day vs. recovery ride — the debate is over" — show the AI prescribing actual rest when you're overreaching

## Influencer Collab Angles
- Send the app to mid-tier cycling creators (10k-50k followers) and ask them to use it for 2-4 weeks, then share honest reactions. The "I didn't expect an AI to actually be good at this" reaction is gold.
- Have them screen-record asking the coach something specific to their goals — every cyclist's plan looks different, which proves personalization
- "Can an AI coach get me to my first century?" — multi-part series with a creator

## Hooks That Stop the Scroll
- "This app just told me not to ride today and it was right"
- "I replaced my $250/month coach with AI"
- "My AI coach knows I'm overtrained before I do"
- "I told my AI coach about my race and it rebuilt my entire week"

## Tactical Advice
- Target cycling hashtags but also broader fitness ones — #cyclinglife #zwift #indoorcycling #biketraining #roadcycling
- The rescheduling/move workout feature is one of the most demo-able features — the conversational back-and-forth of "can we move this to Wednesday" feels magical on video
- Vertical screen recordings of the mobile app work perfectly for reels — the dark theme looks great on phone screens
- Post consistently at 6-7am and 5-6pm — that's when cyclists are checking their phones before/after rides

## Core Message
**"Coaching that adapts to your life, not the other way around."** That's the pain point every self-coached cyclist feels.
